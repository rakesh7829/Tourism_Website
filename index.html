About Shimla & Manali

Shimla and Manali are two popular tourist destinations in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Shimla, the capital city of Himachal Pradesh, is known for its pleasant climate and picturesque views. Manali, on the other hand, is famous for its scenic beauty and adventure sports.
